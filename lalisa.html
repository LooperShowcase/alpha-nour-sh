Lalisa

Lalisa Manobal,
known mononymously as Lisa,
is a Thai rapper,
singer, and dancer based in South Korea.
She is a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink formed by YG Entertainment.
Lisa made her solo debut with her single album Lalisa in September 2021.
